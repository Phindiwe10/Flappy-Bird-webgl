How to Play

🐤 Press Space to make the bird flap.
🛑 Avoid pipes – if you hit one, it's game over.
🎯 Try to pass between as many pipes as possible to increase your score.
🕹 Use the Restart button after a game over.
🔇 Use Mute/Unmute to control sounds.
✅ Press Ready to begin the countdown and start the game!
